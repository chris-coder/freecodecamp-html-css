THANOS

Lord & Saviour who tried to save the half !!

  • Thanos was one of the last sons of A'Lars, progenitor of the second colony of Eternals on Titan, and Sui-San, the last survivor of the original settlement of Eternals in this moon.
  • He was born with purple, hide-like skin and a massive body due to his Deviant Syndrome.
  • By the time he was a teenager, Thanos excelled academically and learned faster than he was taught.
  • Thanos experimented on his own body, granting himself a variety of powers. Thanos' confidante became his companion, and eventually declared that for her to love Thanos, he would have to become a god, a supreme destroyer above all else.
  • Thanos soon formulated a plan to regain Death's affection: he would give to "her" more than any single living being had given "her," the annihilation of everything that lives.
